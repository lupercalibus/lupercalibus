METASTORYTELLING

The story of how this Story is told

IDEA

Data Visualization is used in storytelling mainly to talk about sports, politics and economics. The idea is to use this technique to tell the story of a historical character.

THE STORY

The story of Francesco Baracca is basically a story of a pilot, a war story. In some ways telling the story of a military pilot is similar to telling the story of a sports driver.

CAREER GRAPH
(GeoTime Data Storytelling)

In the studied bibliography, the life and successes of Baracca are always summarized through a table. It was decided to follow this narrative style and improve it thanks to digital media.

First, data of 34 Baracca victories were collected in json format. Later a visualization of the dataset was created: a card for each Baracca victory and a section dedicated to text of pilot's lettes describing each battle.

This Dataset Storytelling Platform focus on 3 factors:

  • TIME: the data has been arranged along a timeline
  • SPACE: every victory is depicted on the map
  • ACTION: a carousel allows space-time navigation

INFOGRAPHICS

Once the dataset was completed and displayed, it was possible to proceed with data analysis and production of statistics to create the graphics. Sports storytelling is based primarily on infographics, so it was a great source of inspiration.

Formula 1

Formula 1 use on board camera technique to make spectator feel inside the car, seeing what the driver sees.

MotoGP

Like Formula 1, also MotoGP uses on board camera technique. In addition, information on the driver ahead are provided.

Enemies Fate Graph

Enemies Fate Graph was created using on board camera graphic, simulating Baracca's point of view and making scene more dramatic. Furthermore, taking inspiration from MotoGP, a donut chart was added on the rival pilot showing probability (calculated on our dataset) of what will happen.
